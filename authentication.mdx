# Your account is your starting point

Your account is where your Explorer Elite journey begins. It helps you sign in, shape your profile, and move through the site with the right role and the right preferences.

<Callout kind="info">
Keep your account secure and up to date so your profile always reflects the journey you want to share.
</Callout>

## Get ready

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your role">
    Decide whether you are joining as an Explorer or a Partner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your profile">
    Make sure your name, picture, and public details feel right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your public profile preferences">
    Choose what people can see and how you want to appear.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore or publish listings">
    Explorers start discovering; Partners start sharing opportunities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track your Passport progress">
    Watch your stamps, achievements, and progress grow over time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you can control

<Cards>
  <Card title="Public profile visibility" icon="eye" horizontal>
    Decide how much of your profile should be visible to others.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Passport sharing options" icon="bookmark" horizontal>
    Choose how your Explorer Passport is presented when you connect with others.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Following and community connections" icon="users" horizontal>
    Keep track of the people and places you want to stay connected to.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Preferences" icon="sliders" horizontal>
    Set the small choices that make the experience feel more personal.
  </Card>
</Cards>
