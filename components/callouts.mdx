# Helpful notes that stand out

Callouts are short blocks that help readers notice something important without losing the flow of the page. In Explorer Elite, they are a simple way to show reminders, helpful nudges, and things to watch for.

## Common callout types

- **Prerequisite**: use when readers need something ready before they continue.
- **Tip**: use when a small suggestion will make the journey easier.
- **Heads up**: use when the reader should pause and pay attention.
- **Success**: use when you want to confirm what good progress looks like.
- **Need help**: use when the reader should know where to go next.

## Best practices

- Keep the message short.
- Use one callout for one idea.
- Choose the clearest kind for the moment.
- Use callouts to support the page, not replace it.
