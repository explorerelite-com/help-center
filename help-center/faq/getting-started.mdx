# Getting started questions

If you are new here, these answers will help you begin with confidence. Choose the tab that matches your role.

<Callout kind="info">
Not sure where to begin? Start with Quickstart, then come back here for quick answers.
</Callout>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Explorer FAQs">
    ### How do I get started as an Explorer?
    Start with your profile, confirm your Explorer Passport, and begin exploring what interests you.

    ### What do Explorer Passport stamps mean?
    Stamps show the experiences you’ve completed and help tell the story of your journey.

    ### Where do I find opportunities?
    Use Quickstart and the main product pages to discover what feels right for you.

    ### How do referrals and rewards work?
    They help you share Explorer Elite with others and recognize growth along the way.

    ### How does my public profile work?
    Your profile helps others recognize you, follow your journey, and understand what you enjoy.

    ### What should I do if something feels missing?
    Check the Help Center pages, then return to Quickstart if you want the big picture again.

    ### How is magazine content used?
    Stories and updates help you discover experiences and stay connected to the community.

    ### How do I get support?
    Use the Help Center for troubleshooting, guides, and next steps.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Partner FAQs">
    ### How do I begin as a Partner?
    Start by confirming your Partner presence and making sure your profile feels clear and welcoming.

    ### What does the Explorer Passport mean for me?
    It helps you understand how Explorers move through the journey and what they value.

    ### Where do I share opportunities?
    Use the partner guide to shape and share what you offer.

    ### How do referrals and rewards work?
    They help you see how people discover and share your presence over time.

    ### What should I show in my public profile?
    Keep it simple, clear, and helpful so Explorers know what to expect.

    ### What if a page or detail seems unclear?
    Visit the Help Center or Troubleshooting page for a clearer path.

    ### How do stories and content help?
    Magazine content gives context and helps people understand your offering.

    ### How do I get support?
    Start with the Help Center, then use guides when you want step-by-step help.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
