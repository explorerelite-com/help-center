# Set up your Partner presence

This guide helps Partners shape a clear public presence, share what they offer, and begin welcoming Explorers with confidence.

<Callout kind="info">
You’ll need: your profile basics, what you want to offer, your public description, and your visibility preferences.
</Callout>

## Follow these steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create or confirm your Partner profile">
    Make sure your profile clearly represents who you are and what you want people to know.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define what you’re offering">
    Decide which listings, experiences, or opportunities you want Explorers to discover.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add details Explorers care about">
    Keep the description simple, helpful, and easy to scan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish and launch">
    Make your presence visible so people can find you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite Explorers">
    Share your presence with people who may be interested in what you offer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track progress over time">
    Watch for growth, referrals, and repeat interest as people engage with your presence.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Success checklist

<Callout kind="tip">
You’re ready when your profile feels clear, your offering is visible, and Explorers can understand what you do at a glance.
</Callout>

- Your profile is complete.
- Your offering is easy to understand.
- Your visibility preferences feel right.
- You know where to check for progress.
