# What to do when something feels stuck

This page helps you work through common questions one step at a time. Choose the tab that matches your role, then follow the scenario that feels closest to your problem.

<Callout kind="info">
Start with the simplest fix first. If it still does not feel right, move to the next step or check the Help Center.
</Callout>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Explorer">
    ## Passport progress not reflecting as expected
    ### What to try first
    Refresh your view, return to your profile, and check whether the change had time to appear.
    ### If it still doesn’t work
    Look at your recent activity and make sure you’re viewing the right profile.
    ### Where to look next
    Quickstart and the FAQs.

    ## Can’t find a listing
    ### What to try first
    Go back to the Marketplace and look for a more general category or nearby path.
    ### If it still doesn’t work
    Check whether the listing was saved, shared, or moved.
    ### Where to look next
    Concepts and Quickstart.

    ## Profile visibility feels confusing
    ### What to try first
    Review your account settings and public profile preferences.
    ### If it still doesn’t work
    Compare what you expect to share with what you want to keep private.
    ### Where to look next
    Account & Access and FAQs.

    ## Referral or rewards questions
    ### What to try first
    Check the page where you first saw the reward or invitation.
    ### If it still doesn’t work
    Revisit the notice or changelog for any recent updates.
    ### Where to look next
    Notices and Changelog.

    ## Community connection questions
    ### What to try first
    Look at who you follow and whether the connection is shown where you expect.
    ### If it still doesn’t work
    Return to your profile and confirm your community preferences.
    ### Where to look next
    Account & Access and Help Center.

    ## Magazine or content access confusion
    ### What to try first
    Go back to the Help Center and open the story or page from there.
    ### If it still doesn’t work
    Check whether you are in the right section for Explorers.
    ### Where to look next
    Help Center and Quickstart.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Partner">
    ## Your presence is not showing as expected
    ### What to try first
    Return to your Partner guide and confirm the basic details are complete.
    ### If it still doesn’t work
    Revisit your public profile and make sure the offer is easy to understand.
    ### Where to look next
    Guide: Set Up Your Partner Presence.

    ## Explorers can’t seem to find your listing
    ### What to try first
    Check that your opportunity is visible and clearly described.
    ### If it still doesn’t work
    Make the title and summary simpler so it is easier to spot.
    ### Where to look next
    Concepts and Quickstart.

    ## Profile visibility or public details feel off
    ### What to try first
    Review what you show publicly versus what you keep private.
    ### If it still doesn’t work
    Compare your presence with the expectations in the Partner guide.
    ### Where to look next
    Account & Access.

    ## Referral or rewards questions
    ### What to try first
    Check whether the reward note or update has already been explained in Notices.
    ### If it still doesn’t work
    Open the Changelog to see if anything changed recently.
    ### Where to look next
    Notices and Changelog.

    ## Community connection questions
    ### What to try first
    Review how Explorers engage with your presence and where the connection may have changed.
    ### If it still doesn’t work
    Make sure your profile and description still invite the right audience.
    ### Where to look next
    Help Center and Guide: Set Up Your Partner Presence.

    ## Magazine or content access confusion
    ### What to try first
    Visit the Help Center and follow the page that best matches the topic.
    ### If it still doesn’t work
    Check whether the content is meant for Explorers or Partners.
    ### Where to look next
    Help Center and FAQs.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
