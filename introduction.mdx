## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create or confirm your Explorer Elite profile">
    Start with one profile that represents your journey.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore or list opportunities">
    Explorers discover what fits them; Partners share what they offer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn stamps, achievements, and points">
    Your Passport grows as you participate, connect, and complete experiences.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Keep building your journey">
    Return often to discover more, share more, and grow your presence.
  </Step>
</Steps>
