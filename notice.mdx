# Why notices exist

Notices are short reminders that help people understand what matters right now. They keep Explorers and Partners aware of community expectations, shared updates, and anything that affects how the product feels to use.

<Callout kind="info">
Notices are meant to be clear and friendly, not formal. Think of them as a simple way to stay aligned.
</Callout>

## Community Guidelines

<Callout kind="alert">
Be respectful, keep communication helpful, and treat other people’s journeys with care.
</Callout>

## Safety and respect

<Callout kind="alert">
If something feels unsafe, unclear, or uncomfortable, pause and use the Help Center to find the next step.
</Callout>
