# Start fast, then grow

This quickstart shows the first things to do as an Explorer or a Partner. Follow the path that fits you, then use the rest of the guide when you want more detail.

<Callout kind="info">
Just follow the steps—no technical setup required.
</Callout>

## Choose your path

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Explorers">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Set up your profile">
        Confirm your Explorer Passport and make your profile feel like you.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Find adventures or opportunities">
        Browse what interests you and save the ones you want to return to.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Engage with content and community">
        Read stories, follow people you like, and join the conversation.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Begin earning stamps">
        Take part in experiences and watch your Explorer Passport grow.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Partners">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Set up your Partner presence">
        Make sure your public presence tells people who you are and what you offer.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Publish or list opportunities">
        Share the experiences, services, or adventures you want Explorers to discover.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Invite Explorers to discover you">
        Help the right people find your listing and understand why it matters.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Track rewards and referrals">
        Keep an eye on the growth that comes from people sharing and returning.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
